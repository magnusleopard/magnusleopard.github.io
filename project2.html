Turret

Home / portfolio / Turret

Some wireframe renders.

This is a sci-fi turret that I created for one of my projects. I had a lot of fun making it, as I got to come up with a design myself and also I love sci-fi stuff.

In the project, I focused more on asset creation and I built this particular one as a hero asset. I tried to do as much kit bashing as possible to save time making parts, because of the limited time frame. All together there are 20 4k UDIMs.

    Software used:

  • Maya
  • Substance Painter
  • Nuke
  • Arnold
