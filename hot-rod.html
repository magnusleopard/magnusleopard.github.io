Hot Rod

Personal project

A high poly version of a Mad Max inspired car (Hot rod). A large part of this car I modeled from a tutorial, that was created by James Schauf. (Video can be found on the Gnomon Workshop site). Most of the time, I used the tutorial as reference and I tried to come up with different ways to model parts myself, while keeping the topology as nice as possible. Other than that, I also learned quite a lot about cars and guns as well, by just looking at other references/videos and understanding, how things actually work. For me it was by far the biggest project, as the car has so many different parts, modeling + unwrapping + texturing all of them, took a lot of time. I used over 20 udims with 4k textures. (RIP my laptop with only 16gb of RAM haha. Can't count the amount of crashes. Definitely learned something about ram management though.) Overall it was a very fun project for me. The looks and the ideas behind those cars are just awesome and crazy at the same time!

    Software used:

  • Maya
  • Substance Painter
  • Nuke
  • Arnold
