Halo Spartan Laser

Personal project

This is the M6 G/GNR Spartan Laser from the Halo games.

I wanted to keep it similar to the weapon from Halo Reach / Halo 4, because I like the older art style more. I started this project quite a while ago but recently decided to finish it. Really enjoyed making it and I'm pretty happy, how it came out in the end.

    Software used:

  • Maya
  • Substance Painter
  • Nuke
  • Arnold
